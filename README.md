# E-Commerce Customer Analysis

## Project Overview
Analysis of Brazilian e-commerce customer data (99,441 customers, R$ 16M revenue)
to understand customer segments, behavior, and retention opportunities.

## Key Findings

### Critical Insight
**100% of customers are one-time buyers** - This represents a major retention gap 
and opportunity for growth.

### Top Insights
- **Customer Concentration**: Top 20% of customers generate 80% of revenue
- **Category Performance**: Beauty/Health and Watches dominate (R$ 1.2M each)
- **Geographic Gap**: São Paulo dominates; North/Northeast underserved
- **Segmentation**: High/Mid/Low value customers evenly distributed

## Recommendations
1. Retention program: Convert 15% of one-time to repeat customers
2. Geographic expansion to underserved regions
3. Category-based cross-selling strategy
4. Segment-based personalization

## Conclusion
This analysis reveals both strength (concentrated high-value customers) and 
critical weakness (zero repeat purchases). The primary opportunity is implementing 
a retention strategy to convert one-time buyers into repeat customers, with 
potential for 15-20% revenue growth.
